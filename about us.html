Our History

"Welcome to OMA RESTAURANTS, where our story begins with a passion for exceptional food and a dream to create a culinary haven. Founded in 2023, our journey started when MMESOMA,a beautiful lady with a love for Cooking, envisioned a place where flavors would dance on the taste buds and guests would feel the warmth of hospitality. The concept of OMA RESTAURANTS took shape during MMESOMA's extensive culinary travels, drawing inspiration from diverse cultures and ingredients. The dream became a reality when MMESOMA gathered a team of talented chefs, each contributing a unique skill to the kitchen. After months of meticulous planning and design, OMA RESTAURANTS opened its doors in NOVEMBER 2023. From the rustic charm of the decor to the carefully curated menu, every detail reflects our commitment to providing a memorable dining experience. Our dedication to quality, creativity, and community has quickly made us a local and favorite. As we continue to grow, our mission remains the same: to delight your senses, celebrate culinary artistry, and become an integral part of the vibrant tapestry of our community. Join us on this gastronomic journey as we write the next chapter of OMA RESTAURANTS's delicious history."
